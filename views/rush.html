FAQs

What is Theta Tau?

Theta Tau is a professional co-ed engineering fraternity. We are a brotherhood that promotes professionalism, service, and strong brotherly ties.

What is rush?

Rush allows you to learn more about Theta Tau and what we have to offer. There will be several events that shows you what our fraternity is about and gives you a chance to meet our active members. Rush is free and isn't a requirement but it will give a better opportunity to learn about the fraternity and increase your chances of receiving a bid.

Why should I rush?

Rush is an opportunity that will allow you to gauge your interest in a professional fraternity.

What are the requirements to join Theta Tau?

You must have an academic GPA of 2.0 or above.

Do I have to be an engineer to rush?

No! We will be accepting Applied Mathematics. We will also accept you if you're intending on becoming an engineer.

What is pledging?

Pledging is a process where you will be initiated into the fraternity. A big part of the pledging process is the development of professional skills, service to the community, and getting to know our members. We can't reveal too much info so you'll have to pledge to find out. :)

Do you have anymore questions?

Contact our rush chair and they will answer your questions ASAP!
